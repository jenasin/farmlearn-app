# FarmLearn - All Questions (75 total)

## BEANS (15 questions)

### Level 1 - Basics
1. What type of crop are beans? → **A legume crop**
2. How do beans help the soil? → **They add nitrogen to the soil**
3. At what depth should beans be planted? → **3-5 centimeters**
4. How many days do beans take to mature? → **60-90 days**
5. What types of beans exist? → **Climbing and bush varieties**

### Level 2 - Growing
6. What fertilizer is best for beans? → **More phosphorus than nitrogen**
7. What does angular leaf spot disease affect? → **Leaves - creates angular spots**
8. Which pests attack beans? → **Aphids, whiteflies, and caterpillars**
9. How should beans be watered? → **Moderately - not too much or too little**
10. What is bean inoculation? → **Adding nitrogen-fixing bacteria to seeds**

### Level 3 - Harvest & Storage
11. How do you know when beans are mature? → **Pods dry out and change color**
12. How are beans harvested? → **Pull or cut plants, then thresh**
13. To what moisture level should beans be dried? → **Below 14%**
14. How are bruchids (storage pests) controlled? → **Proper storage and using ash or pesticides**
15. How do PICS bags help store beans? → **They prevent air and pests from entering**

## CASHEW (15 questions)

### Level 1 - Basics
1. What type of crop is cashew? → **A perennial tree crop**
2. Where does cashew originate from? → **Brazil (South America)**
3. Which part of cashew is edible? → **The nut and the apple (fruit)**
4. How many years can a cashew tree live? → **30-50 years**
5. What climate does cashew thrive in? → **Warm with moderate humidity**

### Level 2 - Planting
6. When is the best time to plant cashew? → **At the start of rainy season**
7. What spacing is needed between cashew trees? → **8-10 meters**
8. What soil is suitable for cashew? → **Sandy soil with good drainage**
9. What size should the planting hole be for cashew? → **60cm x 60cm x 60cm**
10. What should be done before planting cashew seedlings? → **Dig hole and add manure 2 weeks before**

### Level 3 - Care & Harvest
11. How often should cashew be watered during dry season? → **1-2 times per week**
12. What does pruning cashew help with? → **Increasing yield and tree health**
13. When is the best time to prune cashew? → **After harvest**
14. What fertilizer is suitable for cashew? → **NPK and manure**
15. How many years before cashew starts producing? → **3-5 years**

## DROUGHT (15 questions)

### Level 1 - Understanding Drought
1. What is drought? → **A long period without enough rain**
2. How does drought affect crops? → **Plants wilt and die**
3. What is the first sign of drought stress in plants? → **Leaves wilt and curl**
4. How long can drought last? → **Weeks, months, or even years**
5. How can farmers know drought is coming? → **Listen to weather forecasts**

### Level 2 - Water Management
6. How does mulching help during drought? → **Retains moisture in the soil**
7. What materials are good for mulching? → **Dry leaves, grass, or husks**
8. When is the best time to water during drought? → **Morning or evening**
9. How is rainwater harvesting done? → **Collecting water from roofs into tanks**
10. What is the benefit of drip irrigation? → **Uses little water but efficiently**

### Level 3 - Drought-Resistant Crops
11. Which crop tolerates drought well? → **Sorghum**
12. Why are cowpeas good during drought? → **They tolerate drought and enrich soil**
13. Why does cassava tolerate drought? → **Large roots that store water**
14. What type of maize is suitable for drought areas? → **Early-maturing and drought-tolerant varieties**
15. What is the benefit of mixed cropping during drought? → **Reduces risk - if one fails, another survives**

## MAIZE (15 questions)

### Level 1 - Basics
1. What type of soil does maize need? → **Fertile soil with good drainage**
2. When is the best time to plant maize? → **At the start of rainy season**
3. What spacing is needed between maize rows? → **75-90 centimeters**
4. How much rainfall does maize need? → **500-800 millimeters per season**
5. How many days does maize take to mature? → **90-120 days**

### Level 2 - Growing
6. What fertilizer is suitable for maize? → **DAP at planting, CAN later**
7. When should the first weeding be done? → **2-3 weeks after germination**
8. Which pests attack maize? → **Stalk borer**
9. What are signs of nitrogen deficiency? → **Lower leaves turn yellow**
10. How does planting maize in rows help? → **Easier weeding and harvesting**

### Level 3 - Harvest & Storage
11. How do you know when maize is mature? → **Leaves dry out and kernels harden**
12. To what moisture level should maize be dried? → **Below 13%**
13. What does good maize storage require? → **Dry, clean store with ventilation**
14. Which pest damages stored maize? → **Weevil**
15. How does crop rotation help? → **Reduces diseases and improves soil**

## TOMATO (15 questions)

### Level 1 - Basics
1. How much sunlight do tomatoes need? → **6-8 hours of sun per day**
2. How are tomatoes planted? → **First in nursery, then transplanted**
3. What spacing is needed between tomato plants? → **45-60 centimeters**
4. How should tomatoes be watered? → **Regularly but not excessively**
5. How many weeks before tomatoes start producing? → **8-12 weeks after transplanting**

### Level 2 - Growing
6. How does blight disease affect tomatoes? → **Leaves and fruits rot**
7. How does staking help tomatoes? → **Supports plants and prevents diseases**
8. What fertilizer is suitable for tomatoes? → **NPK and manure**
9. How does pruning help tomatoes? → **Increases fruit size**
10. Which pests attack tomatoes? → **Aphids, whiteflies, and caterpillars**

### Level 3 - Harvest & Storage
11. How do you know when tomatoes are ripe? → **Red or yellow color depending on variety**
12. How should tomatoes be stored? → **In a cool, dry place**
13. How is bacterial wilt spread? → **Through contaminated soil and water**
14. How does a greenhouse help tomatoes? → **Controls climate and pests**
15. What causes blossom end rot? → **Calcium deficiency**
